Faylav is the official mobile application for convenient access to the Faylav platform. 

This app provides a seamless experience to access all Faylav services right from your mobile device without the need to open a browser.

Features:
• Easy Login: Quick access to your Faylav account
• Session Persistence: Stay logged in between sessions
• Offline Detection: Notifies you when your connection is lost
• Simple Navigation: Easy to use interface with proper back navigation

The app requires internet access to connect to Faylav services but doesn't collect any additional data from your device.
